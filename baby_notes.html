Baby, I Have Your Back

  • Notes
  • 2024
  • Print
  • 100 x 85 mm

Kindly supported by Cultuur Eindhoven and Fonds Kwadraat

The series of notes accompany each collaborative night walk for the work baby, i have your back. The notes were printed with NPN Drukkers in Breda, the Netherlands. The notes were digitally printed on munken polar paper 80 grams. Thirty copies were printed.
