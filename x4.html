X4 Design Group Internship

  • Branding & Illustration
  • 2018

During my internship at X4 Design Group in Seoul, Korea, I proposed and presented logo designs, character designs, motion graphics and story graphics. Seen are logos, character designs and story graphics that were accepted preliminary designs. I proposed logos for the Seoul Museum of Craft Arts, Enter, and Orrange. Enter and Orrange are electric companies responsible for the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity.
