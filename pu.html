PIN–UP Magazine Sun Issue

  • Design
  • 2020
  • Print

For the Sun issue of PIN–UP Magazine, a magazine for architectural entertainment based in New York, I worked on the design along with art director Erin Knutson and creative director Felix Burrichter. I contributed to the design of the Provence and Enzo Mari spreads.
