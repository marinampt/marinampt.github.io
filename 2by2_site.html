2by2

  • Website
  • 2025

the 2by2 website was designed and developed in-house. i worked on the structure, coding, and overall web development to create a site that functions both as a digital business card and a short introduction to our values and way of working.

2by2 is a collaboration built around balance and creativity. we work in twos, with complementary strengths, but always share tasks and develop ideas together. the website reflects this. it’s split into two sides that play off each other, like an orange ball bouncing back and forth in motion. each side holds space for a perspective, but they’re always in dialogue.

on mobile, the structure mirrors this idea with a vertical orientation, reimagining the same back-and-forth rhythm in a new format.

The website was hand coded by me. Check out the code in GitHub.
