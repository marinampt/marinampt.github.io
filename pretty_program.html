Pretty in Pink on a Dark Street

  • In collaboration with dance artist Anastasia Bezruchko
  • Program
  • 2022

These programs archive the performance Pretty in Pink on a Dark Street, made in collaboration with dance artist Anastasia Bezruchko. The programs were riso printed at Onomatopee in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.
