Baby, I Have Your Back

in collaboration with photographer Aline Papenheim, costume designer Laura Fernández Antolín and walking partner Max Oosterholt

  • Poster
  • 2024
  • Offset Printing
  • 594 x 841 mm

Kindly supported by Cultuur Eindhoven and Fonds Kwadraat

These posters promote a series of collaborative night walks made throughout the Netherlands. For each walk, the posters are customized to describe the walk, location, starting point, etc. The posters were printed with NPN Drukkers in Breda, the Netherlands and were offset printed. one hundred copies were printed and placed near the starting point of each walk. The posters were printed on Munken polar paper 80 grams.
