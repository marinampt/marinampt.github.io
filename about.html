Marina Menéndez-Pidal is a designer focused on clarifying information and experimenting in design and the performing arts. She is Spanish American who was born in Madrid, Spain in 1993 and raised in California, USA. She received her MA in information design at Design Academy Eindhoven in 2022. She has worked in San Francisco and New York, and currently works as a designer in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Her practice is known for representing complex subjects. In her work, she places people at the center of the design process. She finds ways where design can help start conversations. She believes in working with others and making experiments, finding how it works out, and editing the process.
