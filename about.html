Marina is an artist with a background in graphic design. She holds an MA in Information Design from Design Academy Eindhoven in 2022. Previously, she has worked as a designer at Dubberly Design Office and PIN–UP.

She is interested in making an artistic nightwalking practice. Her mission is to share love and light through the streets at night. She makes work not only behind a screen but also through walking, drawing, and engaging with spaces. She makes work at night alone, with friends and artists. This practice is sustained by a curiosity about the night and the everyday. She loves learning, growing and evolving and is open for conversations and collaborations.
