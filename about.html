Marina Menéndez-Pidal is an artist / designer currently in Eindhoven, the Netherlands. She is Spanish American who was born in Madrid, Spain in 1993 and raised in California, USA. She received her MA in Information Design at Design Academy Eindhoven in 2022. Previously, she has worked as a designer at Dubberly Design Office and PIN–UP.

As a designer, she is interested in working with artists, small businesses, and cultural institutions on projects that communicate ideas across different media: books, documents, printed matter, identities, websites, and physical spaces.

Her artistic practice explores the movement of walking and the potential of walking as a way to research. This practice is kept alive by a curiosity about places, people, and movement. Some themes she has been exploring lately include femininity and safety when walking in public space. She is interested in communicating her findings in collaboration with dance artists and performers. She believes in working, growing, and learning with people, making new discoveries and experiments, and valuing creativity and process.
