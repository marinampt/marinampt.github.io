Marina Menéndez-Pidal is a designer currently based in Eindhoven, the Netherlands. She is Spanish American who was born in Madrid, Spain in 1993 and raised in California, USA. She received her MA in Information Design at Design Academy Eindhoven in 2022. Previously, she has worked as a designer at Dubberly Design Office and PIN–UP.

As a designer, she is interested in making services useful and delightful through design. She is open for full-time and freelance design work. When she is not designing, she enjoys reading, voguing, and drawing.
