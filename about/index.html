Marina is an artist with a background in graphic design. She holds an MA in Information Design from Design Academy Eindhoven in 2022. Previously, she has worked as a designer at Dubberly Design Office and PIN–UP.

She is interested in making a collaborative practice that shares love and light through the streets at night. She makes work at night alone, with friends, and with artists. She would like to remain anonymous. on a daily basis, she works not only behind a screen but also through walking, drawing, and engaging with different spaces. The work is supported by a curiosity about the night and the everyday. She loves learning, growing, and evolving, and she is open to conversations and collaborations.
