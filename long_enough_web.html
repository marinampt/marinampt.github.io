We have kept our secret long enough

  • Web archive
  • 2023

This site archives a series of walks in high heels from January 2023 in and around Eindhoven, the Netherlands. The walks are ongoing. This work dives into the joys, dreams, pains and discomforts of being feminine and walking in high heels and opens those experiences to an audience during Dutch Design Week 2023. Over the course of many months, I accumulated forty walks. I noted the day, time, weather, temperature, route, attire, makeup, posture, pace, thoughts and encounters before, during and after the walk.

The web archive was hand coded using HTML, CSS and Javascript. It is currently being updated. Check back soon!
