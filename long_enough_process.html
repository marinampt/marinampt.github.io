We have kept our secret long enough

  • Development Process
  • January–August 2023

this work began from a curiosity about walking in high heels. i was given a pair of dries van noten heels by dance artist anastasia bezruchko and became very curious and excited about walking in them. i created a simple system to document my experience walking in high heels in eindhoven. i recorded the weather, temperature, route, posture, pace, attire, makeup, thoughts, and encounters from each walk. from january to august 2023, i documented forty walks. i have walked in the rain, snow, and sunshine. i have walked short distances (from the end of my street) to very long distances (from tilburg central station to my home in eindhoven). i have walked in and around eindhoven at different times of the day and night. i simply brought a pencil and paper with me and documented before, during, and after each walk. matilde stolfa helped me photograph the process and document the experience.
