baby, i have your back

  • Web invite
  • 2025

to announce the four queer bar night walk, i made a website invitation to introduce the work and invite anyone who felt called to join. the site offered a doorway into the spirit of the night walk, open and playful.

after the night walks, the site became an evolving archive. it now holds traces of all eight night walks we organized, unfolding like a walk in the park: a slow, continuous scroll where images appear and fade at the natural pace of walking. an invitation to linger and to move through the night once again.

The website was hand coded using HTML and CSS. It is currently being updated. Check back soon!
