Crop Kiosk

Crop is a kiosk focusing on the everyday healthy foods we eat: fruits and vegetables. The food is freshly picked from the farms, and are chopped and served to the customer.

The final logo contains two halves: the fruity top and the roots holding where it came from.

The ceiling depicts the cropped fruits and the pole holding the top of the kiosk mimic the qualities of the logo.
