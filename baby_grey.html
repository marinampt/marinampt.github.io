Baby, I have your back at The Grey Space in the Middle

  • Performance
  • fall 2024–winter 2025
  • Photography: Aline Papenheim
  • Walking partner: Max Oosterholt
  • Costume designer: Laura Fernández Antolín
  • Locations: The Grey Space in the Middle,
    • Café Saarein, Bar Buka, Lellebel and Lola the Green Aardvark
  • Role: Concept, content, organization, design and direction

  • Kindly Supported by Amarte

    • Thank you so much to The Grey Space in the Middle, Café Saarein, Bar Buka, Lellebel, Lola the Green Aardvark and Amarte for helping bring this work to life.

For walk seven, At The Grey Space in the Middle, walking partner max and i got ready and shared the process with the public. as max and i headed outside, i wrote the information about the night walk on their back while max wrote the memory on my back. for this walk, i had the idea of getting ready in complete darkness. Aline was the light. We circled around the center of the Hague and ended at The Grey Space in the Middle.

For walk eight, walking partner max and i walked between four queer bars in amsterdam: café saarein, lellebel, bar buka and lola the green aardvark. we got ready in the spaces, shared the process and had some energizing conversations with people. thank you to everyone who helped bring this work to life.
