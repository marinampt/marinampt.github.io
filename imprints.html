Imprints & Breadcrumbs

  • SEA Foundation x Odapark
  • Social Media Posts
  • 2025
  • 1080 x 1080 px
  • visual identity by emese veszely

these social media posts introduce the exhibition imprints & breadcrumbs, which explores the physical in relation to memory. the show features the work of three artists. i designed the digital posts using the given visual identity as a starting point. drawing from the concept of breadcrumbs, i created imprints that became part of the visual language. these shapes added a new layer to the identity and allowed for playful, dynamic compositions.
