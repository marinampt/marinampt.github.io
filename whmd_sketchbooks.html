we’re here, my dear

  • sketchbooks
  • 2025

  • bound at no kiss
  • textile & holmen trnd 70 gsm
  • 64 pages
  • 6 sketchbooks for night team
  • 26 sketchbooks for the public
  • 9.9 × 14 cm

these sketchbooks are part of the work we’re here, my dear. each performer in the night team writes or draws inside them, and the outcomes are later embroidered into the costumes. to connect the sketchbooks to the costumes, leftover fabric from the costume production was used to make the sketchbook covers. since the fabrics were remnants, each cover is unique in its shape, color, and size.
