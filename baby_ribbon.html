for the final night walk—walk eight, we night walked between four queer bars in amsterdam. our team invited anyone to join. someone is open to tie the moment to their walking partner using a custom ribbon and step out into the night and keep each other close. the ribbons were printed at dpn rikken on a thick, rough textile.
