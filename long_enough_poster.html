We have kept our secret long enough

  • in collaboration with dance artist Anastasia Bezruchko and sound artist iota
  • Poster
  • 2023
  • Offset printing
  • 420 x 594 mm

These posters promoted the live performace We have kept our secret long enough during Dutch Design Week 2023. The process in making the posters were simple: I went on a few walks and documented elements of the streets in Eindhoven, which became the backbone of the poster. I played with negative and positive space and the intense feeling when walking in high heels.

The posters were printed on 115 g/m2 magno silk paper and were printed with Drukkerij Tielen.
