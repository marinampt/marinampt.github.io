Baby, I Have Your Back

  • Motion Graphic
  • 2024
  • Projection
  • 1920 x 1080 px

Kindly supported by Cultuur Eindhoven and Fonds Kwadraat

This motion graphic introduces the work baby, I have your back. it was projected in restaurant nor in eindhoven may 18, 2024. the initial storyboard and layout of the motion graphic resembles broadway lights. i also played with the idea of an eye test i took when i was very young. i distinctly remembered a dot floating about a space and had the idea of the dot filling around the space to become a line. this then became the main layout for the motion graphic and ties nicely with the poster and notes.
