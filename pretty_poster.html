Pretty in Pink on a Dark Street

  • in collaboration with dance artist Anastasia Bezruchko
  • Poster
  • 2022
  • Print
  • 210 x 297 mm

These posters promote the performance, Pretty in Pink on a Dark Street, during the graduation show at Design Academy Eindhoven. The posters were made in collaboration with dance artist Anastasia Bezruchko and were riso printed at Onomatopee in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.
