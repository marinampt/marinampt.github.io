Night walks

  • Poster
  • 210 × 297 mm
  • 2022

these posters initiated night walk interviews in and around eindhoven. after putting up the posters at design academy eindhoven, i conducted fifteen night walk interviews with women, trans women, and feminine-presenting people. when making the poster, i went on several night walks alone and archived the lines I gathered, which became the backbone of the poster. the placement of the text was done intuitively.
