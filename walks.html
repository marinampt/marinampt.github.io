Night walks

  • Poster
  • 2022
  • Silkscreen print
  • 210 x 297 mm

These posters initiated night walk interviews in and around Eindhoven. After putting up the posters in Design Academy Eindhoven, I conducted fifteen night walk interviews with women, transwomen and feminine presenting people. When making the poster, I went on a few night walks by myself and archived the lines, which became the backbone of the poster. I took an intuitive approach with where the text went on the poster.
