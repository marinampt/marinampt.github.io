Sex and Care

  • Diagram
  • 2021
  • Print
  • 3 × 2 m

Related to

  • Sex and Care, live performance

Sex workers provide sexual services in exchange for money or goods. Sex and Care is a diagram for education and care for the sex worker with hopes to bring people together across borders, care practices, and knowledge worlds. Sex and Care explores the rights of a sex worker to redefine our ethical position of the profession. Imbalanced power structures, transphobia, sexism and racial discrimination are still common in the sex work industry. It is time for an upgrade that meets the needs of contemporary society through education, awareness, and sensitivity to the marginalized group.
