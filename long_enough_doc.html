We have kept our secret long enough

  • Document
  • 148 × 210 mm
  • 2023

This document archives a series of walks in high heels from January to August 2023 and dives into the joys, dreams, pains and discomforts of being feminine and walking in high heels in Eindhoven, the Netherlands. To see the initial part of the research process, check it out here.

Paper includes 115 g/m2 magno silk and 280 g/m2 invercote G. The documents were printed with Drukkerij Tielen.
