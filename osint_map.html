OSINT For Ukraine application map

  • Document
  • 2023
  • 1 x 2.5 m
  • Designed for OSINT for Ukraine
  • Role: Hierarchy, content, organization and design

Using the application mapping method from Dubberly Design Office, I began by making an application map for the initial research for OSINT for Ukraine and their website.

Here, I have mapped and modeled OSINT for Ukraine’s existing website in order to understand the structure of the organization and content. OSINT for Ukraine is an independent (non-profit) initiative of young professionals dedicated to use Open Source Intelligence to investigate International crimes committed in Ukraine, Influence and Disinformation operations in Europe, and provide basic and advanced OSINT courses and training. The collective was founded by a multinational team of professionals who possess knowledge and experience in OSINT Investigation, Investigative Journalism and International criminal, Humanitarian and Human Rights Law.
